About Curtivelle

At Curtivelle, we believe curtains are not just window coverings—they are a defining element of interior storytelling. Based in the heart of Dhaka, Bangladesh, we are a luxury curtain brand dedicated to crafting elegance, comfort, and timeless design for refined residential and commercial spaces.

From premium fabrics to bespoke curtain solutions, every piece we create is designed to elevate interiors and reflect the lifestyle of those who value detail, quality, and sophistication. Whether it’s a modern apartment, a luxury villa, a boutique hotel, or a corporate space, Curtivelle transforms windows into statements of style.

More About Us

Our mission

We are a luxury curtain brand built for modern living. Not mass-produced. Not copy-paste designs. We’re obsessed with quality, proportion, texture, and how light interacts with fabric throughout the day.

Curtivelle was created to fill a gap in the market—curtains that match the sophistication of today’s interiors. Homes are evolving. Offices are becoming more experience-driven. Hospitality spaces are raising the bar. We exist to support that evolution with curtain solutions that feel tailored, thoughtful, and future-ready.

Our team is a mix of designers, fabric specialists, craftsmen, and consultants who understand both aesthetics and function. We don’t chase trends blindly—we interpret them, refine them, and make them work for real spaces in Bangladesh’s climate and lifestyle.

Our Curtain Collection

At Curtivelle, our curtain collections are designed to match lifestyles, spaces, and seasons. Our Working Process

Good design doesn’t happen by accident. Our process is structured, transparent, and client-focused—so you always know what you’re getting.

Consultation & Assessment

We start by understanding your space. Our experts evaluate measurements, lighting conditions, wall finishes, furniture layout, and usage patterns.

Design & Fabric Selection

Based on your interior style and needs, we recommend fabric types, colors, curtain styles, and hardware options. You’ll see samples, feel textures, and visualize the outcome before production begins.

Custom Production

Once approved, our skilled craftsmen bring the design to life with precision stitching, perfect pleats, and flawless finishing.

Installation & Styling

Our professional installation team ensures clean, accurate fitting. We don’t rush this step—because execution matters just as much as design.

Our professionals

Behind Curtivelle is a leadership team driven by creativity, precision, and a deep understanding of interior design trends. Our leaders bring together experience in textiles, design consultancy, and customer service, ensuring every project is handled with professionalism and care.
